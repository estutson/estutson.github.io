About

I am a aspiring .NET developer currently attending 1150 Academy in Carmel, Indiana. After looking at numerous fields such as Informatics, IT, and Mechanical Engineering, I was instantly hooked when it came to Software Development.

After much research I became convinced coding schools or "bootcamps" were the quicker and more in depth path to a coding career versuses the average four year degree. I quickly found 1150 Academy, and was introduced to their ALP 9 week accelerated program. This program focuses on multiple languages including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, and applies them to real world applications.
